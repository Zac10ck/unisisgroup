The Story

A Journey of Two Decades

From humble beginnings to a global conglomerate, every milestone tells a story of vision, perseverance, and excellence.

2003

The Beginning

Raju Kurian arrives in Dubai with a vision to build something extraordinary. The foundation of Unisis Group is laid, starting the journey of a diversified business empire.

2008

Healthcare Revolution

MEDAS Middle East Software Systems LLC is established, recognizing the immense potential in healthcare technology. The company begins transforming patient care across the GCC region.

250+ Healthcare Implementations
2009

Engineering Excellence

Unisis Engineering is born, expanding into oil & gas, construction, and industrial services. Partnerships formed with major players across Saudi Arabia and the Gulf region.

2010

Global Logistics Network

PSL Arabia Shipping & Logistics launches, becoming a trailblazer in Middle East freight forwarding. Operations span Saudi Arabia, UAE, Kuwait, Bahrain, and India.

2011

Nurturing Future Engineers

VISAT Engineering College opens its doors in Kerala, India. A dream to give back to the community and nurture the next generation of engineers becomes reality.

APJ Abdul Kalam University Affiliated
2012

Democratizing Education

TutorComp is founded with a vision to make quality education accessible globally. The platform connects students with expert tutors across 23 countries.

100,000+ Sessions Delivered
2018

15 Years of Excellence

A grand celebration at Gulf Hotel, Bahrain marks 15 years of Unisis Engineering. Industry leaders and partners gather to celebrate this remarkable milestone.

2023

Two Decades of Impact

Unisis Group celebrates 20 years of excellence with operations spanning 7 countries, 7 subsidiary companies, and over 1000 dedicated team members worldwide.

The Future

Continuing the Legacy

With innovation at our core and excellence as our standard, we continue to expand, transform industries, and create lasting impact across the globe.

The Visionary

Raju Kurian

Chairman & Managing Director

An innovator, entrepreneur, and visionary leader with over 20 years of experience in building and scaling businesses across multiple industries and continents.

From his early days as an employee in the UAE to becoming the architect of a global business empire, Raju Kurian's journey exemplifies the power of vision, determination, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Under his leadership, Unisis Group has grown from a single venture into a diversified conglomerate with presence in healthcare technology, education, engineering, logistics, and IT solutions.

" Our commitment is to bring innovative ideas to the industries we represent, adding value to people's lives while maintaining the highest standards of quality and integrity. "

Our Companies

Seven industry-leading companies, each a testament to innovation and excellence.

01

MEDAS

Healthcare Software Solutions

Established in 2008, MEDAS is a complete healthcare software solution provider that has revolutionized patient care across the GCC region. With 250+ implementations in hospitals, clinics, and healthcare facilities, MEDAS delivers comprehensive ERP solutions integrating clinical operations with financial management.

Est. 2008 250+ Implementations GCC Wide
02

TutorComp

Global Online Tutoring

Award-winning personalized online tutoring platform connecting K-12 and university students with expert educators worldwide. Operating across American, British, Australian, and CBSE curricula. Academic Partner of the Government of Dubai.

Est. 2011 100,000+ Sessions 23 Countries
03

PSL Arabia

Shipping & Logistics

A trailblazer in the Middle East logistics industry, PSL Arabia provides comprehensive freight forwarding solutions including air, sea, and road freight, customs clearance, warehousing, and project logistics for oil & gas, pharmaceuticals, and e-commerce sectors.

14+ Years 5 Countries Full-Service
04

Unisis Engineering

Industrial Services

Providing industrial engineering solutions for oil & gas, construction, and manufacturing sectors. Celebrating 15+ years of excellence with partnerships across the Gulf region and recognition from major industry players.
